InstaCart-Clone

InstaCart-Clone is a clone of the InstaCart website. InstaCart is an American company that operates as a same-day grocery delivery and pick-up service in the U.S. and Canada. Customers can order groceries from participating retailers with the shopping being done by a personal shopper. The service is available via a website and mobile app.
